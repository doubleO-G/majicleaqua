]]> We are excited to announce Oriplast’s association with the highly anticipated Bengali-language thriller film, “Dasham Avatar (The Tenth Avatar).” Directed by the acclaimed Srijit Mukherji and produced by Jio Studios and SVF Entertainment, this film is set to make waves in the Bengali cinema scene.

 

“Dasham Avatar” boasts an impressive cast, including Prosenjit Chatterjee, Anirban Bhattacharya, Jisshu Sengupta, Subhashree Ganguly, and Jaya Ahsan. This cop multiverse film is set to premiere in India on October 19, 2023.

 

While the plot of “Dasham Avatar” is currently shrouded in secrecy, it is known to explore the intriguing concept of multiple versions of the same characters from different universes. Additionally, it is rumored to serve as a prequel to Srijit Mukherji’s previous hits, “Baishe Srabon” and “Vinci Da.”

 

The stellar cast includes:

  • Prosenjit Chatterjee in the role of Dy.SP Abhijit Pakrashi

  • Anirban Bhattacharya as Sub-Inspector Kabir Khan

  • Jisshu Sengupta portraying DIG Arnab Roy

  • Subhashree Ganguly as Dr. Rini Sen

  • Jaya Ahsan in the role of DCP Nandini Mukherjee

 

The creative team behind this cinematic venture includes:

 

  • Director: Srijit Mukherji

  • Producer: Shibashish Sarkar

  • Writer: Srijit Mukherji

  • Music Composer: Anupam Roy

  • Cinematographer: Swapnil Sonawane

  • Editor: Aniket Avinash

 

Mark your calendars because “Dasham Avatar” is all set to hit the screens on October 19, 2023. This film is generating immense anticipation in the Bengali film industry, primarily due to the reunion of the dynamic duo, Srijit Mukherji and Prosenjit Chatterjee, known for their successful collaborations on films like “Baishe Srabon” and “Vinci Da.” Furthermore, the multiverse concept promises to bring a fresh and exciting dimension to Bengali cinema, creating a buzz that’s sure to captivate audiences. Stay tuned for an extraordinary cinematic experience brought to you by Oriplast and “Dasham Avatar.”

]]> Ladies and gentlemen, prepare yourselves for an extraordinary cinematic experience! The Eken, Ruddhaswas Rajasthan is a sensational Bengali-language detective crime film that is sure to leave you on the edge of your seat. 

Directed by the incredible Joydeep Mukherjee and produced by the masterful duo of Shrikant Mohta and Mahendra Soni under the esteemed banner of Shree Venkatesh Films, this film is the second installment of the thrilling Eken franchise.

Get ready to witness the mesmerizing performance of the talented Anirban Chakrabarti as he dons the role of the legendary Ekenbabu, a fictional detective created by the brilliant Bengali author, Sujan Dasgupta. 

The screenplay, written by the exceptional Padmanabha Dasgupta, is sure to keep you spellbound throughout the entire film.

Mark your calendars, folks! The Eken, Ruddhaswas Rajasthan is set to take the world by storm on 14th April, the auspicious occasion of Poila Boishakh, the Bengali New Year. 

The story revolves around Ekendra Sen, who, while enjoying his vacation in the vibrant and colorful state of Rajasthan with his friends Bapi and Pramatha, is suddenly drawn into a thrilling investigation. 

A professor of archaeology at Oxford University, Shatadru Ghosh, approaches Eken Babu with his suspicion that a particular idol he had seen in London has gone missing.

Eken Babu, accompanied by his trusted confidants, sets out on a quest to uncover the truth behind the missing idol. Along the way, they encounter a series of mind-boggling mysteries that will challenge Eken’s intelligence and observation skills at every turn. 

Get ready to be swept away on a rollercoaster of emotions as Eken Babu unravels the web of deception and intrigue in this gripping crime thriller.

Don’t miss out on this blockbuster hit, folks! The Eken, Ruddhaswas Rajasthan is a masterpiece that is sure to leave you breathless with its stunning visuals, gripping storyline, and outstanding performances. So sit back, relax, and get ready for a cinematic adventure like no other!
